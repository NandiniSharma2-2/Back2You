## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Cyan**: #00F0FF (Main branding color)
- **Primary Pink**: #FF007F (Accent color)
- **Success Green**: #39FF14 (Success states)
- **Warning Gold**: #FFD700 (Warnings)
- **Dark Background**: #0A0F1D (Primary background)
- **Card Background**: rgba(255,255,255,0.1) (Glass effect)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable fonts
- **UI Elements**: Consistent sizing and spacing

### Interactive Elements
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with hover effects
- **Cards**: Glass-morphism with subtle shadows
- **Forms**: Dark backgrounds with cyan borders
- **Animations**: Smooth Framer Motion transitions
